About Leica 0-Series Photography Studios

Leica 0-Series Photography Studios is a local studio named after Leica O-Series camera brand produced in 1923 that broke records to become the most expensive camera ever sold. The model is one of only 25 ever produced, which were created as test devices two years before the launch of the first official Leica release.

Therefore, the Leica 0-series Photography Studios is named in honour and remembrance of its founder Ernst Leitz.
